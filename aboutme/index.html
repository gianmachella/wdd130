I am from Caracas Venezuela, since I was little I have been interested in drawing and Stake, I grew up with my grandmothers, my mother and my aunts, at 17 years old I learned about the gospel and a few weeks later, to the one who would be the love of my life, I was baptized some weeks later and the following year I went on a full-time mission in the great Venezuela Mission - Barcelona, ​​I returned with honors and 6 months later I was sealed in a holy temple in Caracas Venezuela, we have been married for almost 16 years and have 3 beautiful children, Gianna 14 years old, Franco 11 years old and Luca (the Argentinian) 3 years old, we lived for 6 years in Buenos Aires Argentina and since February of this year we have been living in Dallas TX.

I love skating and playing the guitar, I learned to play the guitar during my time as a missionary, I studied Graphic Design for a few years but discovered my passion in programming years later.
